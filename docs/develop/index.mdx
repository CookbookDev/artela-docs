# 🔍 Overview

Artela is an [EVM](https://ethereum.org/en/developers/docs/evm/) compatible blockchain network built with [Cosmos SDK](https://v1.cosmos.network/sdk). You can seamlessly deploy your Ethereum dApps on Artela without hassle. And at the mean time, you can also leverage your dApp with Aspect programming to make it even more powerful.
